Popular Cities

Bengaluru

The capital city of Karnataka, known as the Silicon Valley of India. Famous for IT companies, startups, nightlife, and parks.

Population: 1.3 Crore | Famous: Tech Hub

Mysuru

Known for Mysore Palace, Dasara festival, silk sarees, sandalwood, and royal heritage.

Population: 12 Lakh | Famous: Palace City

Mangaluru

Beautiful coastal city known for beaches, ports, seafood, and educational institutions.

Population: 6 Lakh | Famous: Port City

Hubballi

Major commercial center of North Karnataka, known for industries and transportation.

Population: 10 Lakh | Famous: Business Hub

Belagavi

Important city near Maharashtra border known for industries, military establishments, and pleasant weather.

Population: 8 Lakh | Famous: Industrial City

Kalaburagi

Historic city known for forts, mosques, educational institutes, and rich culture.

Population: 7 Lakh | Famous: Heritage City

About Karnataka

Karnataka is one of the most vibrant states in India, blending modern cities with ancient culture and natural beauty.

It is famous for technology, heritage sites, beaches, wildlife, hill stations, waterfalls, temples, and delicious cuisine.

From Bengaluru's innovation to Mysuru's royal history and Mangaluru's coastal charm, Karnataka offers something for everyone.
